About


COVID-19-Watch is a public online tool to provide direct access to local, easy-to-understand COVID-19 data.


Key Features:

  • Find and share - Quickly find and share local COVID-19 status reports for anywhere in the nation
  • Change over time - Explore change in COVID-19 data over time
  • Local, state, and national - Explore data and trends at local, state, and national levels
  • Up-to-date data - Data is pulled daily from Johns Hopkins

Find and Share

Find a COVID-19 Status Report by using the search bar to search the U.S., or type "USA" for the national Status Report. You can also click the map to see the local or state Status Report.

Use the search bar or click the map to find a COVID-19 Status Report

Status Reports are catered to specific regions

You can send a direct link to a particular Status Report using the URL at the bottom

Change Over Time

Use the "play" button and time-slider in the bottom-right to step back in time, allowing you to view historical data back to February 2020. The map and Status Reports are coordinated to change with the date.

Local, State, and National

Search for a town, city, or county to get a local Status Report. You can also search for a state or search "USA" for a national status report.
